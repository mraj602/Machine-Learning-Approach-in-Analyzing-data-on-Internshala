About InternPred

Many internship seekers find it difficult to take up an internship relevant to their skills and technologies known. With over thousands of internships available in Internshala, it becomes a hectic task to sort out the best job opportunities and know where one stands. InternPred aims to provide internship seekers with estimated stipend based on their skills, experience and other relevant data. It brings out user specialized key insights from all the internships available on the website publicly. It also aims at bringing out the generalized idea of current stipend offerings at industry level.
