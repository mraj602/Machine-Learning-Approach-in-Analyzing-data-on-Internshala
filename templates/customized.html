Customized Analysis

Know everything about Internshala - According to what suits you!

You want to know what stipend you deserve? This page provides a platform to analyse the suitable internships based on your preferences. The page asks for the following details:

  • Skills: You can enter the skills you have as an applicant. There can be many combinations of skills. Note that entering a lot of skills might lower your estimated stipend as the algorithm gives estimates based on all the skills entered. You can tweak with multiple options to understand the estimate.
  • Perks: Different companies on Internshala offer different perks. Choose the perks that you want to get from the company. Note that most of the companies offer for "5 Days of the week" perk. Therefore, choosing "None" might actually lower your estimated stipend. You can tweak with multiple options to understand the best estimates.
  • Category: This offers the list of categories offered on Internshala. The algorithm does not take into account this data to estimate your stipend. It is for an applicant to understand the different categories present on Internshala. Filling the field is optional.
  • Location: It is an important factor to look after while estimating an internship. If you want to compare different locations, choose "Find the best one".
  • Duration: Stipends are highly variable to duration of internship seeking. In general trend, lower durations offer low stipends but the same is the case with higher durations. Tweak with different values to understand the best estimate.
  • Important Note: The estimated values are not actual values and they should be used in a way to quantify the different options available on Internshala. These values are a great way to compare the data on Internshala but should not be used as a benchmark to decide on internships. Different internships with the same skills and locations might have different stipends due to other factors not accounted for in the analysis.
  • Important Note: These estimations are based on data available on Internshala. As location and duration are the most important feature for estimating the stipend, you might get the same estimated values for a set of duration and locations with different skills. This is because each location has a minimum standard stipend that is offered for any kind of internship irrespective of the skills you possess.
  • You are good to go to estimate stipends based on your preferences. However, if you want to understand how estimations are made in order to get a more clear idea, you should visit our github page.
Important Note on Data Sharing: We save the data entered by you for internal analysis only. In no way we spam your email or share your data with anyone.
