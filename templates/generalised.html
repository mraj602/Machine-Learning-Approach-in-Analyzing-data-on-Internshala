Generalised Analysis

Know everything about Internshala - The companies they offer, their stipends, job location, skillset and many more...

Overview

Generalised analysis provides an overall analysis of data available throughout the Internshala. This analysis gives you an idea of what the companies offer to you and where you should put your shot to land an internship. This analysis helps you find out the current industry trend based on different parameters present on Internshala!
This analysis is based on 8569 internship offerings on Internshala. Note that the number of internships on Internshala changes from time to time.
If you want to know more, visit our Customized Analysis page!

Categories of internships offered

This graph shows the share of internships on Internshala based on the category of an internship. In general, content writing-related internships are widely available on Internshala. To understand this graph, for example, out of 8569 internships analyzed, 178 internships are of UI/UX, which is approx 2% of the total internships available.

Internships based on durations

Relation between Duration and Stipend

Different duration of internships offers different stipends. To analyze this relation, this Kernal Density graph is used. The darker the region, the more internships are available in that region. To understand this graph, for example, most internships are available for a 3-month duration which offers a stipend of around 5000. Few more analysis about Internshala...

  • 2.98% of internships on Internshala are unpaid internships. These consist of postings by NGOs, government organizations, etc. These can be useful to start with if you are not landing on any paid internships.
  • 15.82% of internships on Internshala do not require any specific skills.
  • Perks on Internshala are a major factor in deciding a stipend for an internship. As opposed to what it may seem, companies not giving perks are actually low-paying internships.
  • 2194 unique categories of internships are available on Internshala.
  • 386 unique skills are available on Internshala for an applicant to find internships
  • 393 unique locations are available on Internshala to apply for an internship
